# FlowPilot Impact Model

## Enterprise Assumptions

| Parameter | Value | Source |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Company size | 500 employees | Mid-size enterprise benchmark |
| Meetings per person per week | 8 | Atlassian Workplace Survey 2024 |
| Action items per meeting | 3 | Internal research average |
| Current follow-through rate | 30% | Harvard Business Review estimate |
| FlowPilot follow-through rate | 85% | Based on automated tracking + escalation |
| Average hourly cost | $50 | Blended rate (engineering + management) |
| Weeks per year | 48 | Excluding holidays |

## Time Savings

### Manual Note-Taking Elimination
- Time saved: 2 hours/person/week (manual notes, formatting, distributing)
- Total: 500 x 2 x 48 = **48,000 hours/year**
- Dollar value: 48,000 x $50 = **$2.4M/year**

### Follow-Up Chasing Elimination
- Time saved: 1.5 hours/person/week (checking on action items, sending reminders)
- Total: 500 x 1.5 x 48 = **36,000 hours/year**
- Dollar value: 36,000 x $50 = **$1.8M/year**

### Meeting Redundancy Reduction
- Repeat meetings (because actions were not done): 20% of total meetings
- Total meetings/week: 500 x 8 = 4,000
- Meetings eliminated: 4,000 x 0.20 = 800/week
- Average meeting: 45 minutes, 4 participants
- Hours saved: 800 x 0.75 x 4 = 2,400 hours/week
- Annual: 2,400 x 48 = **115,200 hours/year**
- Dollar value: 115,200 x $50 = **$5.76M/year** (partial -- not all would be eliminated)
- Conservative estimate (30% capture): **$1.73M/year**

### Total Annual Savings: **$5.93M/year**

## Per-Employee Impact

- Hours saved per employee per year: (48,000 + 36,000) / 500 = **168 hours/year**
- That is **4.2 work-weeks** returned to each employee annually
- Equivalent to hiring 84 additional FTEs without any recruitment cost
